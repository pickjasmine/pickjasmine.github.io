Zanzibar's mission is to bring fresh and delicious coffee to the people.


Our coffee is roasted in house, giving it a unique and refreshing flavor that will delight your tastebuds.
We also make and serve fresh sandwiches, salads, soups, and a variety of baked goods that feature seasonal flavors.
We believe in supporting our community by featuring local performance artists and buying from area farmers and producers.

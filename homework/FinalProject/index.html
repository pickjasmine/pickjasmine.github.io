Zanzibar's mission is to bring fresh and delicious coffee to the people. Our coffee is roasted in-house, giving it a unique and refreshing flavor that will delight your tastebuds.

We also make and serve fresh sandwiches, salads, soups, and a variety of baked goods that feature seasonal flavors.

At Zanzibar's, we believe in supporting our community.

We host live performances every Saturday night, featuring prominent bands and performers from around the St.Paul and Minneapolis area.

We buy ingredients for our soups, sandwiches, and other delicacies from local farmers and producers.

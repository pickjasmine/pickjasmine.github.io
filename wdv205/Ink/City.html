We’ve been custom designing landscapes and gardens in the Des Moines area since 2005. Our tree nursery hosts over 4 acres of trees, shrubs and landscaping services. We have a full greenhouse containing perennials and annuals to supply three seasons of custom gardens. In our showroom you will find all the materials you need to build your own outdoor cooking center.

Our History

City Gardens & Landscaping started in 2005 by founder Mr. Joe Boxelder. We started from humble beginnings, running two landscaping crews, and operating a small retail nursery. As the City of Des Moines grew, so did we. We have serviced over 125 homes and business centers in Central Iowa.

Our Staff

Over the years, City Gardens & Landscaping has tripled both in size, and in products offered. We’ve been under the same name and ownership for over 10 years. City Gardens & Landscaping now employees 30-40 employees, and carries the largest selection of plant materials in the area. We have strong ties to the local community, and have decades of landscape history and community service work here.

We are listed in The Iowa Mall!

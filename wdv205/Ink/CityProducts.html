Landscaping & Gardens

City Gardens & Landscaping has tackled just about every kind of landscaping project you can imagine. If it involves the outdoors, we can design it. We also have the added benefit of carrying our own top quality plants and landscape supplies. Our full service Nursery and Garden Center allows YOU to select what goes into your project. We’re always happy to get your input before we begin.
